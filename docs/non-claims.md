# Tau Non-Claims

Tau is a zero-trust agentic harness and evidence control plane. It does not
certify legal, compliance, security, or operational readiness.

Tau does not prove ITAR compliance.

Tau does not replace export-control counsel.

Tau does not replace an empowered official.

Tau does not prove legal sufficiency.

Tau does not certify Embry-OS.

Tau does not certify Sparta Explorer.

Tau does not certify Kimi, scillm, or any local model.

Tau does not prove ATO readiness.

Tau does not prove SCIF readiness.

Tau does not authorize use on controlled technical data.

Tau provides review support through:

- policy and data-boundary gates;
- typed receipts;
- evidence manifests;
- DAG containment;
- local provider routing evidence;
- human approval gates;
- reviewable proof chains.

Every external-review demo must remain synthetic unless a human with the right
authority approves a separate controlled-data boundary.
