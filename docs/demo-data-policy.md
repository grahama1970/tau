# Demo Data Policy

All external-review demos for Tau, Embry-OS, and Sparta Explorer must use
synthetic data only.

Allowed:

- invented contract clauses;
- invented F-36/SPARTA-like records clearly marked synthetic;
- invented QRA, control, finding, and blocker IDs;
- local fake artifacts;
- mock export-control scenarios;
- fixture receipts that are explicitly labeled as fixture evidence.

Forbidden:

- real ITAR data;
- real controlled technical data;
- real customer data;
- real Lockheed, GE, DARPA, supplier, or government internal data;
- real classified, CUI, proprietary, or export-controlled records;
- copied proprietary contract language;
- public GitHub mutation from the airgap demo;
- external network calls in the airgap profile;
- chat-authored compliance or signoff verdicts.

Synthetic examples may mention export-control concepts only as invented demo
content. They must not be presented as legal classification, export-control
approval, or operational authorization.
