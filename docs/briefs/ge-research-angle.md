# GE Research Review Angle

Tau is positioned as an agentic evidence harness for industrial review,
inspection, quality, and audit workflows.

What to inspect:

- agents can help draft extraction, crosswalk, and review artifacts;
- Tau keeps claims separate from evidence;
- Sparta Explorer can render blockers, evidence freshness, and proof chains;
- Embry-OS can host local memory, provider, monitor, and evidence services;
- humans retain approval authority for signoff and external release.

The synthetic airgap demo should be treated as a pattern for reducing audit
drag, not as proof of compliance automation or plant readiness.
